Project Overview

This project demonstrates the management of a building refurbishment and retrofit project from planning through to completion. The project includes strip-out works, structural alterations, MEP upgrades, internal fit-out, testing, and final handover.

Refurbishment Retrofit Planning & Control RAMS Cost Management

Scope of Works

  • Pre-construction surveys and investigations
  • Site setup and temporary works
  • Strip-out and enabling works
  • Structural and fabric upgrades
  • Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) upgrades
  • Internal refurbishment works
  • Testing, commissioning and inspections
  • Snagging and project handover

Lessons Learned

  • Careful planning is essential when refurbishing existing buildings
  • Early identification of existing services reduces construction risks
  • Programme monitoring helps manage delays effectively
  • Clear documentation supports safe site operations and smooth handover
